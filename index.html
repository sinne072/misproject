Hello, my name is Kayli Sinner and I'm a Marketing and Consumer Insights and Analytics major, with a minor in Psychology at the University of Minnesota Duluth! I'm currently a sophmore and on track to graduate in the Spring of 2028.

I love to adventure and explore new places. Some families go to Florida or Hawaii, but my family goes camping around Lake Superior. The vacations we take are spent up North in campgrounds. I’ve been all over the Midwest. Some of my favorite places are Madeline Island, Pattison State Park, Tettegouche State Park, Jay Cooke State Park, Ashland, WI, Door County, and Copper Harbor, MI. I also enjoy going on motorcycle trips with my dad. Our most recent trip was riding around Lake Superior. We went over the 4th of July weekend, and when we got back, our total mileage for the whole trip was 1776 miles. What a crazy coincidence!

One of my passions is creating art in any form. I like to take photos, collage, decorate cakes, make and paint pottery, draw, paint rocks, and paint canvases. I have been painting since I was very young, and while I don’t get to do it all the time, now that I’m in college, I love any free time I get to paint something beautiful. I take inspiration from my love for sailboats, lighthouses, lobsters, flowers, fruit, and other things in nature. I like using oil paints the most and am inspired by Monet and his paintings.
