Why UMD?

I chose UMD because I love the North Shore. I've been coming up here since I was three and so it means a lot to me. Now that I go to college here, I only see Duluth as a college town. The whole tourism aspect has dissapeared. I love UMD's business school and all the diffrent ways you can connect with the community.

I'm only a sophmore, but I feel like I've made this place my home. I really would'nt have it any other way. I had an amazing frehsman year, I met my four best firends, I joined clubs, and went on lots of adventures. As a sophmore, I've only continued to grow and try new things. I'm very busy now, but I'm gratful for that. I'm glad my life is filled with the things I love.
